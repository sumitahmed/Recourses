# Active Passive

## Overview

A focused, original practice module for **Active Passive** in the english track. Use it after a short concept review, then log every error in the portal.

## Concepts

Read for grammar, meaning, and context together. In Active Passive, explain why the chosen form is correct rather than relying on what merely sounds familiar.

## Important Formulae

Use a short rule card: subject–verb agreement, tense sequence, voice, diction, or contextual meaning as appropriate.

## Tricks

- Estimate first; exact work should confirm the estimate.
- Underline the quantity or relationship the question actually asks for.
- Use a clean scratch layout so mistakes are easy to diagnose.

## Solved Examples

**Example.** Apply the core idea to a small case before attempting timed questions.

**Method.** Identify the input, apply one valid rule at a time, and verify the result against the question.
